Recycling at Arnhem Festival

Reusable cups

At the Arnhem Festival, reusable cups will be used because it is better for the climat. To encourage everyone to reuse them a fee of 1 euro will be charged which you can retrieve later on.

Handing in cups

There are 2 ways to retrieve the fee of your cup

  • When ordering a new drink you don't have to pay a fee if you hand in your empty cup
  • You can hand in your cup at the coupon stand to receive 1 euro back for your empty cup
